Pizza

Ingredients (for 2 pizzas):

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • ¾ cup warm water (adjust as needed)
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 100g processed cheese, grated
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ capsicum (bell pepper), thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced (low juice preferred)
  • 4 mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chili flakes

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl. Dissolve yeast in warm water with a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 10–15 minutes until frothy. Add yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rise for 4–6 hours.
  2. Make the base: Preheat oven to 180°C (356°F). Roll out the dough into a circular shape. Prick with a fork to prevent puffing. Bake for 10 minutes.
  3. Add toppings and bake the pizza: Spread tomato sauce over the base. Add processed cheese, veggies, and mozzarella. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes. Bake at 250°C (482°F) for 10 minutes or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
