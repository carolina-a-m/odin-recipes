Chicken curry recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 split chicken breasts
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 5 medium carrots, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 Thai red chili peppers (optional for heat)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1½ tbsp mild curry powder
  • 2 tsp flour
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1½ tsp ginger
  • 2 cups hot chicken stock
  • 1 cup table cream
  • 1 tbsp sweet mango chutney

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken, season with salt and pepper, and cook until lightly browned (about 4–5 minutes).
  2. Add onions, carrots, garlic, and chili peppers. Cook until onions are soft (about 5 minutes).
  3. Stir in tomato paste to coat everything evenly.
  4. Add curry powder, flour, turmeric, cumin, and ginger. Mix well until dry.
  5. Pour in chicken stock and bring to a boil. Add cream, return to boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in mango chutney just before serving.
  7. Serve over basmati rice with warm naan bread.
