Chicken tacos recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 small tomato
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Chili powder or fresh chili (optional)
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa (your favorite kind)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the chicken with half an onion, a garlic clove, and salt until fully cooked (about 45 minutes).
  2. Shred the chicken once cooled.
  3. In a pan, sauté chopped onion and garlic in olive oil until golden.
  4. Add cumin, pepper, chili, and tomato. Cook until soft.
  5. Mix in the shredded chicken and a ladle of broth. Simmer until liquid reduces.
  6. Heat tortillas and fill with chicken mixture.
  7. Top with cheese, cilantro, lime juice, and salsa.
